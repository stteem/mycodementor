Learning to code or build software comes with lots of highs and lows. You'd barely bask in the highs when another low sets in, and lows can be depressing when you've been trying to solve a problem for days, it's called a blocker.


There's a general consensus that blockers should not exceed 30 minutes without looking for help. From experience, eliminating blockers makes up for the bulk of our sessions with our mentees. Wouldn't you rather have a mentor to turn to when the going gets tough?
